Size

The clouds can be so large that it can contain several hundreds of stars.

Classes of Nebulae

They can be divided into two classes: dark nebulae, and bright nebulae. Dark nebulae contain up to about half of all interstellar material. Bright nebulae are dense clouds of gas within the diffuse interstellar medium.

What is a Nebula?

A nebula is a massive cloud of gas or dust in space and are the birthplace of new stars.

Star Nursery

Nebulas are the birth place of stars. As the gravity fields collapse, the hydrogen clump together to form a star.
